Ride the Lightning

Metallica

1984

Ride the Lightning is the second studio album by the American heavy metal band Metallica. The album was released on July 27, 1984, by the independent record label Megaforce Records. The album was recorded in just three weeks. The album title was taken from a passage in Stephen King's novel "The Stand".

Band Members

James Hetfield - Guitar, Vocals
Lars Ulrich - Drums
Kirk Hammett - Guitar
